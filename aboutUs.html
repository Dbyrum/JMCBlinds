-About Us

JMC Blinds was founded in the year 2001 by a man named Jose Celaya. The name JMC Blinds was created by combining the names of Jose and his wife Minerva Celaya. Jose started this business so he can provide for his family and still have time for his children. Throughout the years, Jose has shown such dedication and class that he is a well respected business owner known by the community.
